Peace University of Medical Sciences(PUMS)

STUDYING AT PUMS

Peace University of Medical Sciences (PUMS) offers academic opportunities in medical oriented fields. Academic programmes offered by the university span different fields of specialization including Basic Medical Sciences, Basic Clinical Sciences, Dental Sciences, Clinical Sciences, Nursing Sciences, Medical Rehabilitation. The university also has a modern library that is fully computerized. With these facilities PUMS is about the best choice for research-oriented individuals and even the health care focused ones.
