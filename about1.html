About Us


Peace University of Medical Sciences (PUMS), a first grade unique Institution of higher learning, is an autonomous public institution with the general function of providing liberal higher education and research in Nigeria.

The University was established on August 3, 2022 under the old Osun State with the name Peace University of Medical Sciences, Osogbo (PUMS).

PUMS as a university and its staff has won many awards and accolades among which are:

  • Best State University in Nigeria
  • Best University of Medical Sciences in Nigeria
  • 11th best University in Nigeria in terms of research
  • 74th best University in Africa in terms of research.
  • 4th best university in Nigeria.

PUMS has a student population of about thirty thousand (30,000) and has more than three thousand (3,000) highly skilled and internationally acclaimed staff (academic, nonteaching and contract) in its employ.

The quality of staff has consistently reflected in the standard of works being done in PUMS. It has also reflected in the awards and laurels being won regularly by the University.

Today, PUMS offers varying degree programmes in eight faculties of:

  1. Science
  2. Basic Medical Science
  3. Basic Clinical Science
  4. Clinical Science
  5. Dental Science
  6. Nursing Science
  7. Applied Health Science
  8. Medical Rehabilitation
